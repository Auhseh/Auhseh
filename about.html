About me

I'm Victor Akhilome, a product designer with cross-disciplinary experience in Finance and Business Strategy, i genuinely believe you can design to make life better and that great design is the seamless blend of Form and Function.

Before design, i ran a mini hedge fund, started a comic book company and worked in Strategy at a financial media company.

Outside design, i make music as a rapper, songwriter and producer, i loooove basketball and i'm a big Warriors fan. I spend a lot of time reading and sometimes i actually wonder what goes on in Gillian flynn's mind.

I genuinely believe love can change the world, sounds cliche but take a second to think about it, if everyone learned to love each other, we won't have a lot of the problems we have in the world today. It starts with being good to people.
